We are here to develop Nav Yug in India.

Nav Yug Samajic Seva Sansthan contribute immensely to global development by seeking to prove the quality of life for those whom poverty and disadvantage are a daily reality. This is achieved by devoting resources into advocacy campaigns aimed to represent the community and demand the interests of civil society to be recognized.
